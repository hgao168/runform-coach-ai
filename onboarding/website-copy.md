## 3. Features Page — 6 Feature Descriptions

### Feature 1: AI Video Pose Analysis

**EN — AI Video Pose Analysis**
See your running form like never before. Record a short video from three angles (side, back, front) and our Vision AI instantly maps your body's biomechanics. We measure 15+ metrics — cadence, stride length, torso lean, knee lift, arm swing symmetry, hip drop, overstriding risk, and ground contact pattern. Each metric is benchmarked against optimal ranges, so you know exactly what to work on.

**ZH — AI 视频跑姿分析**
以前所未有的方式看清你的跑姿。从三个角度（侧面、背面、正面）录制一段短视频，我们的 Vision AI 即刻映射你身体的生物力学特征。我们测量 15+ 项指标——步频、步幅、躯干前倾角、抬膝高度、摆臂对称性、髋部下坠、过度跨步风险、触地模式等。每一项指标都与最优区间对标，让你精准知道该从哪里改善。

---

### Feature 2: AI Personalized Training Plan

**EN — AI Personalized Training Plan**
Generic plans don't know you have a hip drop. Ours does. RunForm generates training plans that respond to *your* actual form weaknesses. Hip drop? Your plan includes glute stability work. Overstriding? We program cadence drills. Training for a World Marathon Major? We build a complete race-specific block — from base building to taper — with every session justified by your biomechanical data. Not a template. A coach.

**ZH — AI 个性化训练计划**
通用训练计划不知道你有髋部下坠的问题。我们的知道。RunForm 生成的训练计划直接回应你真实的跑姿弱点。髋部下坠？你的计划包含臀肌稳定性训练。过度跨步？我们安排步频专项训练。在备战世界六大满贯？我们为你构建完整的赛前训练周期——从基础期到赛前减量——每一堂课都有你的生物力学数据作为依据。这不是模板，这是教练。

---

### Feature 3: Elite Athlete Comparison

**EN — Compare with the Greats**
Ever wondered how your running form stacks up against Eliud Kipchoge? Now you can find out. Our Compare Mode overlays your biomechanical data against elite marathoners, showing you exactly where the gaps are — and more importantly, what to do about them. Compare yourself against your own history too: see how your form has improved week over week. It's the most motivating (and humbling) feature you'll ever use.

**ZH — 精英运动员对比**
好奇你的跑姿和基普乔格差在哪？现在你可以亲眼看到。我们的对比模式将你的生物力学数据与精英马拉松运动员叠加呈现，清晰展示差距所在——更重要的是，告诉你怎么缩小这些差距。你也可以和过去的自己对比：看看你的跑姿如何一周比一周进步。这将是跑步 App 里最让你有动力（也最让你谦卑）的功能。

---

### Feature 4: Strava Integration

**EN — Strava Sync, Supercharged**
Connect your Strava account and RunForm weaves your training history into every analysis. We look at your weekly mileage, pace trends, and workout load alongside your form data — so our coaching advice is grounded in your real training context. One tap to connect. All your runs, now with biomechanical depth.

**ZH — Strava 数据同步**
连接你的 Strava 账号，RunForm 将你的训练历史融入每一次分析。我们会结合你的周跑量、配速趋势和训练负荷，与跑姿数据一起解读——确保每一条教练建议都基于你真实的训练背景。一键连接，让你的每一次跑步都拥有生物力学的深度解读。

---

### Feature 5: Progress Tracking & Trends

**EN — See Yourself Improve**
Numbers don't lie, and neither do trend lines. Track your form score, cadence consistency, stride efficiency, and injury risk indicators over time. Every analysis is saved to your history, with trend charts that show you getting better — or flag when something's drifting in the wrong direction. Progress you can see builds confidence you can feel.

**ZH — 进步追踪与趋势**
数字不会撒谎，趋势曲线更不会。追踪你的跑姿评分、步频一致性、步幅效率和伤病风险指标的变化。每一次分析都保存在你的历史记录中，趋势图表清晰展示你的进步——或者在你偏离正轨时及时预警。看得见的进步，带来感受得到的信心。

---

### Feature 6: Real-Time Voice Coaching (Coming in v1)

**EN — Real-Time Voice Coach**
Meet your in-ear running coach. As you run, RunForm monitors your cadence, torso angle, and stride mechanics in real time — and whispers guidance through your headphones. "Cadence 165 — pick it up to 175." "You're leaning forward — straighten your trunk." Like having a biomechanist on your run, without breaking your flow. Available in the next release. Join the waitlist.

**ZH — 实时语音教练（v1 即将上线）**
认识你的耳中跑步教练。在你跑步时，RunForm 实时监测你的步频、躯干角度和步幅力学——并通过耳机轻声给出指导。「步频 165，加快到 175。」「你在前倾，挺直躯干。」就像有一位生物力学专家陪你跑步，却不打断你的节奏。下一版本即将上线，加入候补名单。

## 8. Brand Voice Guidelines (Reference for All Copy)

| Dimension | English | 中文 |
|-----------|---------|------|
| **Tone** | Biomechanics-backed, runner-to-runner voice. Warm but precise. Never condescending. | 科学有据，跑者对跑者说话。温和但精准。绝不居高临下。 |
| **Avoid** | Hype words ("revolutionary," "game-changing"), fear-mongering about injuries | 夸大词（"颠覆性"、"革命性"）、伤病恐吓式话术 |
| **Embrace** | Specific numbers (15+ metrics, 3 angles), concrete outcomes, runner empathy | 具体数字（15+ 指标、3 角度）、可感知结果、跑者共情 |
| **Hook** | "me vs Kipchoge" — always mentionable, highly shareable | "我和基普乔格比跑姿" — 天然传播素材 |
