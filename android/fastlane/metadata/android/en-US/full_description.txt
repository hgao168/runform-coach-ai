Science-Backed Biomechanics
RunForm detects overstriding — the #1 cause of knee pain running. It identifies pelvic drop, excessive trunk lean, heel striking, and asymmetries that lead to injury. Understanding your running biomechanics is the key to faster, pain-free miles. Our sensor pipeline uses your phone's accelerometer and gyroscope to deliver cadence tracking within ±2 SPM accuracy and ground contact time estimation.
