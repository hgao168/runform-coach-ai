# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated / 最后更新：2026-05-16**

## English

### 1. Introduction

RunForm Coach AI ("RunForm", "we", "our", or "us") is a running form analysis and coaching application available on iOS, Android, WeChat Mini Program, and Web platforms. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Running Videos
- Videos you upload or record for AI-based running form analysis
- We process the video to extract biomechanical metrics (cadence, vertical oscillation, ground contact time, stride length, etc.)
- **Storage**: Videos are temporarily processed on our servers and automatically deleted after analysis is complete. We do not permanently store your raw video files.

#### 2.2 Motion Sensor Data
- Accelerometer data for real-time cadence detection (WeChat Mini Program / mobile)
- Used only during active detection sessions

#### 2.3 User Profile Information (Optional)
- Name, nickname, running level, weekly mileage, target race/goal, gender, shoe size, leg length, shoe brand/model, injury notes
- Used to personalize training recommendations and set analysis baselines
- **Storage**: Local device storage and cloud synchronization

#### 2.4 Analysis Feedback
- Star ratings and text comments you submit about analysis results
- Used to improve AI model accuracy and user experience

#### 2.5 Device Information (Automatic)
- Device model, operating system version, mini-program base library version
- Used for compatibility testing and crash analysis
- Does **not** include device unique identifiers (IDFA, IMEI, etc.)

#### 2.6 Connected Fitness Platforms (Optional)
- If you connect Strava, we may access your activity summaries
- We do **not** use Strava API data to train AI models
- You can disconnect Strava at any time to revoke access and delete imported data

### 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Form Analysis**: Process running videos to analyze biomechanical metrics
- **Training Recommendations**: Generate personalized training plans and exercises
- **Service Improvement**: Use aggregated, anonymized feedback to improve our AI models
- **Compatibility**: Ensure the app works correctly across different devices and OS versions
- **Communication**: Respond to your support requests and feedback

We do **not**:
- Sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for targeted advertising or user profiling
- Share your analysis results without your explicit consent

### 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

- **Cloud Services**: We use Tencent Cloud (WeChat Cloud Development) for data storage within China, and Railway for API hosting
- **Analytics**: We do not integrate third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Umeng, GrowingIO)
- **Advertising**: We do not use third-party advertising SDKs that track user behavior
- **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose information if required by law

### 5. Data Security

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Videos are deleted immediately after processing (real-time processing mode)
- User profiles and analysis records are stored in secure cloud databases
- Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only

### 6. Data Retention

- **Videos**: Deleted immediately after analysis completion
- **Analysis Records**: Retained while your account is active
- **User Profile**: Retained while your account is active
- **Feedback**: Retained in anonymized form for model improvement
- Upon account deletion, all personal data is deleted within 30 days

### 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your stored personal data through the Profile page
- **Delete**: Clear analysis history at any time (History page one-click delete)
- **Export**: Request export of your analysis data
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke camera/album permissions through system settings
- **Account Deletion**: Request account deletion by contacting runformcoachai@gmail.com

### 8. Children's Privacy

RunForm is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

### 9. Cross-Border Data Transfer

- Data from Chinese users is stored on servers within mainland China
- API processing may occur on servers outside China (Railway, US region), with only anonymized analysis requests transmitted

### 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through in-app notifications and by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of RunForm after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### 11. Contact Us

- **Email**: runformcoachai@gmail.com
- **Support**: See SUPPORT.md for additional contact methods

## 中文

### 1. 引言

RunForm Coach AI（以下简称「RunForm」或「我们」）是一款跑步姿态分析与训练指导应用，覆盖 iOS、Android、微信小程序和 Web 四大平台。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

### 2. 我们收集的信息

#### 2.1 跑步视频
- 您上传或录制的用于 AI 姿态分析的跑步视频
- 我们处理视频以提取生物力学指标（步频、垂直振幅、触地时间、步幅等）
- **存储方式**：视频在服务器上临时处理后即时删除，我们不永久保存原始视频文件

#### 2.2 运动传感器数据
- 加速度计数据，用于实时步频检测（微信小程序/移动端）
- 仅在主动检测期间使用

#### 2.3 用户配置信息（可选）
- 姓名/昵称、跑步水平、周跑量、目标赛事、性别、鞋码、腿长、跑鞋品牌型号、伤病说明
- 用于个性化训练建议和分析基线设定
- **存储方式**：本地设备存储和云端同步

#### 2.4 分析反馈
- 您对分析结果提交的星级评分和文字评论
- 用于改进 AI 模型准确性和用户体验

#### 2.5 设备信息（自动采集）
- 设备型号、操作系统版本、小程序基础库版本
- 用于兼容性适配和崩溃分析
- **不包含**设备唯一标识符（IDFA、IMEI 等）

#### 2.6 第三方运动平台（可选）
- 如您连接 Strava，我们可能访问您的活动摘要
- 我们**不会**使用 Strava API 数据训练 AI 模型
- 您可随时断开 Strava 以撤销授权并删除导入数据

### 3. 信息使用方式

- **姿态分析**：处理跑步视频，分析生物力学指标
- **训练建议**：生成个性化训练计划和练习方案
- **服务改进**：使用汇总的匿名反馈数据改进 AI 模型
- **兼容性**：确保应用在不同设备和系统版本上正常运行
- **沟通**：响应您的支持请求和反馈

我们**不会**：
- 向第三方出售、出租或交易您的个人数据
- 将您的数据用于定向广告或用户画像
- 未经您明确同意分享您的分析结果

### 4. 数据共享与第三方

- **云服务**：使用腾讯云（微信云开发）进行中国大陆境内数据存储，Railway 提供 API 托管
- **分析工具**：不集成第三方数据分析 SDK（如友盟、GrowingIO）
- **广告**：不使用跟踪用户行为的第三方广告 SDK
- **法律合规**：如法律要求，可能依法披露信息

### 5. 数据安全

- 所有数据传输使用 HTTPS 加密
- 视频处理完成后即时删除（实时处理模式）
- 用户配置和分析记录存储于安全的云数据库
- 生产数据访问权限仅限于授权人员

### 6. 数据保留

- **视频**：分析完成后即时删除
- **分析记录**：账户活跃期间保留
- **用户配置**：账户活跃期间保留
- **反馈**：以匿名形式保留用于模型改进

您有权：
- **访问**：通过「我的」页面查看已存储的个人数据
- **删除**：随时清空历史分析记录（历史页一键清空）
- **导出**：申请导出您的分析数据
- **撤回同意**：通过系统设置关闭相机/相册权限
- **注销账户**：联系 runformcoachai@gmail.com 申请账户注销

### 8. 儿童隐私

RunForm 不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如发现误收集，将立即删除。

### 9. 跨境数据传输

- 中国用户数据存储于中国大陆境内服务器

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更将通过应用内通知和更新「最后更新」日期告知用户。变更后继续使用 RunForm 即视为同意更新后的政策。
