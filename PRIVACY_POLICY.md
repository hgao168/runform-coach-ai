# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

RunForm AI respects your privacy.

Information We Collect:

* Running videos uploaded by users
* Basic app usage analytics
* Optional fitness-related data, including connected Strava activity summaries

How We Use Information:

* Analyze running form
* Generate personalized training recommendations
* Improve app performance and features
* Use connected Strava data only for the user's own coaching, training load, and plan generation

We do not use Strava API data to train AI models.

Data Sharing:
We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Storage:
Uploaded videos may be temporarily processed for AI analysis.
You can disconnect Strava in the app to revoke access and delete imported Strava runs and weekly summaries.

Subscriptions:
Any future subscriptions will be managed through Apple App Store billing.
