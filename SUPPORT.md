# Support / 支持

## English

For help with RunForm Coach AI, please contact us:

**Email:** runformcoachai@gmail.com

We aim to respond within 1-2 business days.

### Common Issues

- **Video upload fails**: Ensure file is under 100MB and try compressing. WeChat Mini Program users should grant camera/album permissions.
- **Analysis takes too long**: Processing time depends on video length and server load. Typical analysis completes within 30-60 seconds.
- **Results seem inaccurate**: AI analysis depends on video quality. Ensure good lighting, full body in frame, and shoot from side or rear angle.
- **Cadence detection not working**: Ensure the phone is held firmly or secured at waist/arm. Detection only works when screen is on.
- **Can't connect Strava**: Ensure you've authorized RunForm in your Strava settings. Disconnect and reconnect to refresh.

### 常见问题

- **视频上传失败**：请确保文件小于 100MB，尝试压缩后上传。微信小程序用户请确认已授权相机/相册权限。
- **分析时间过长**：处理时间取决于视频时长和服务器负载，通常 30-60 秒内完成分析。
- **分析结果不准确**：AI 分析依赖视频质量，请确保光线充足、全身入镜、从侧面或后方拍摄。
- **步频检测不工作**：请将手机握紧或固定在腰部/臂带。检测仅在屏幕亮起时工作。
- **无法连接 Strava**：请在 Strava 设置中确认已授权 RunForm。尝试断开重连刷新权限。
