## 中文版

### 你跑了几千公里，但从没「看见」自己怎么跑

跑步是最诚实的运动——公里数不会骗人，配速不会骗人。但有一个东西一直在骗你：**你的自我感觉**。

"我觉得我跑得挺直的。"  
"我步频应该不低吧？"  
"膝盖疼可能是鞋的问题。"

跑步教练圈有一句话：**你感觉到的，和摄像头拍到的，是两个人。**

### 姿态不对，每步都在「还债」

跑步是高重复性运动。一场 10 公里，大约 10,000 步。如果你的每一步都有微小的问题——过度跨步、骨盆下沉、躯干前倾——这一万步就是一万次错误的累积。

常见跑姿问题及后果：

| 问题 | 你感觉 | 实际伤害 |
|------|--------|---------|
| 步频过低 (<160) | "我跑得挺稳" | 膝盖冲击力 ×1.5 |
| 过度跨步 | "步子大跑得快" | 胫骨骨膜炎风险 |
| 骨盆侧倾 | "可能有点晃" | IT Band 综合征 |
| 躯干前倾 | "我核心还行" | 下背痛 |

### You've run thousands of miles — but never 'seen' how you run

Running is the most honest sport. Miles don't lie. Pace doesn't lie. But one thing has been lying to you: **how you think you look when you run.**

"I think my form is pretty straight."  
"My cadence is probably fine."  
"My knee pain is probably the shoes."

There's a saying among running coaches: **What you feel and what a camera captures are two different people.**

### Bad form is a debt you pay with every step

Running is high-repetition. A 10K run is about 10,000 steps. If each step has a tiny flaw — overstriding, hip drop, excessive forward lean — that's 10,000 repetitions of that flaw.

Common issues:

| Issue | You feel | Actual impact |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| Low cadence (<160) | "Feels steady" | 1.5× knee impact |
| Overstriding | "Long strides = fast" | Shin splint risk |
| Hip drop | "Maybe a bit wobbly" | IT Band syndrome |
| Forward lean | "Core is fine" | Lower back pain |
