## Screenshot Design Guidelines

### Brand Colors
- Background: Dark (#0D0D0D or #1A1A2E)
- Accent: Mint Green (#00D4A0)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF)
- Ratings: Green (Good), Yellow (Fair), Red (Needs Work)

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, large (SF Pro / Roboto Bold, 40pt+)
- Body: Regular, medium (SF Pro / Roboto Regular, 22pt)
- Sub-text: Light, smaller (16pt)

### Device Frames
- iOS: Use Apple product images per App Store guidelines
- Android: Use Google Pixel or generic Android device frames on transparent/white background per Google Play guidelines

### Localization
- Screenshots should be localized for each App Store locale
- Chinese screenshots: Replace English overlay text with Chinese equivalents
- Dutch screenshots: Same localization approach
