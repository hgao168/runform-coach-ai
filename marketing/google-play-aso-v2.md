## 三、英文长描述 v2（关键词融入版）

> Google Play 长描述限制 4000 字符。以下为 8 段结构，关键词密度 2-3%。

### 第 1 段：痛点 Hook + 核心词簇（0-150 字符）

**关键词**：`running form coach`, `real-time running coach`, `improve running form`, `injury prevention`, `video running analysis`

```
RunForm is the first real-time AI running form coach that tells you HOW
your body runs — not just how far or fast. Upload a running video for
instant biomechanical analysis of 15+ metrics. Now with LIVE voice
coaching: get real-time form feedback while you run. Improve your
running form before injuries start — not after.
```

### 第 2-3 段：实时语音教练 + 功能展开（150-500 字符）

**关键词**：`voice coaching running`, `audio running coach`, `live running feedback`, `running cadence`, `real time form feedback`

```
NEW in v1: Real-Time Voice Coach
Run with your phone in a belt or armband. RunForm's AI running voice
coach watches your running cadence and posture in real time — and tells
you what to fix through your headphones:

"Your cadence is 162 — try speeding up to 170 steps per minute."
"Your trunk is leaning forward 8 degrees — straighten up."

Like having a personal running coach in your ear. Audio cues are
limited to 5 per run, spaced 15+ seconds apart, so you stay focused
on the run — not on your phone.

RunForm's video analysis detects 15+ running metrics including cadence,
ground contact time, trunk lean angle, vertical oscillation, and pelvic
drop. Get live running feedback on cadence and posture during every run.
```

### 第 4-5 段：差异化 + Me vs Kipchoge（500-1000 字符）

**关键词**：`compare running form`, `running technique`, `running form corrector`, `ai running coach`, `improve running form`

```
Compare Your Form Against Kipchoge
See your running technique side-by-side with marathon legend Eliud
Kipchoge. RunForm's AI running form comparison reveals exactly where
your form differs from elite benchmarks — cadence, stride, posture,
ground contact time, and more. It's the ultimate running form
corrector: you can't fix what you can't see.

As an AI running coach, RunForm creates personalized marathon training
plans based on YOUR actual running form — not generic templates. Whether
you need to improve running form for daily runs or prepare for race day,
your plan adapts to YOUR body's biomechanics.
```

### 第 6-8 段：扩展覆盖 + 长尾词（1000-2500 字符）

**关键词**：`biomechanics running`, `pose running`, `running coach app`, `strava alternative`, `knee pain running`, `prevent running injury`, `cadence tracker running`, `marathon training plan`

```
Science-Backed Biomechanics
RunForm detects overstriding — the #1 cause of knee pain running.
It identifies pelvic drop, excessive trunk lean,heel striking,
and asymmetries that lead to injury. Understanding your running
biomechanics is the key to faster, pain-free miles.

Your Personal Cadence Tracker
Track your cadence over time. See how your running cadence evolves
from 164 to 178 SPM. Every run builds your history — and RunForm
shows your trends.

Looking for a Strava alternative that focuses on form over segments?
RunForm is more than a run tracker — it's your personal running
coach app built on sports science and computer vision.

RunForm is for every runner:
• Beginners wanting to build safe running technique from day one
• Marathoners preparing with a personalized marathon training plan
• Injured runners wanting to prevent running injury recurrence
• Pose running enthusiasts analyzing their form mechanics

Download RunForm and discover what your running form is really doing.
First video analysis is free.
```
